## TEMPLATE 2: ANALYZE A JOURNAL ARTICLE

**When user selects [2], ask:**

### Question 1:
**What is the article? (PMID, DOI, or title)**
(e.g., "35234567" or "NEJM 2024;391(9):812-822")

### Question 2:
**What type of article is it?**
- RCT (Randomized Controlled Trial)
- Meta-analysis
- Observational Study
- Systematic Review
- Case Series

### Once you have answers, EXECUTE:

```
TRAUMA JOURNAL CLUB ANALYSIS

Article: [USER'S CITATION]
Article Type: [USER'S TYPE]

REQUEST: Perform comprehensive journal club analysis of this trauma study.

PROVIDE:

1. STUDY QUALITY ASSESSMENT
   - GRADE rating (High/Moderate/Low/Very Low)
   - Sample size adequacy
   - Randomization quality (if RCT)
   - Blinding method
   - Loss to follow-up rate
   - Bias assessment (selection, performance, detection, attrition)

2. STUDY DESIGN & METHODS
   - Study design
   - Population (n, inclusion/exclusion)
   - Intervention vs control
   - Primary & secondary outcomes
   - Statistical methods

3. RESULTS
   - Primary outcome: effect size, 95% CI, p-value, NNT
   - Secondary outcomes with CI
   - Subgroup analyses
   - Adverse events

4. CLINICAL SIGNIFICANCE
   - Is effect size clinically meaningful?
   - How different from current practice?
   - Real-world impact?
   - Who benefits most?

5. COMPARISON TO GUIDELINES
   - Aligns with ATLS/EAST/WEST?
   - Supports, challenges, or changes guidelines?
   - Should practice change?

6. LIMITATIONS
   - Internal validity threats
   - External validity/generalizability
   - Design limitations

7. PRACTICE IMPLICATIONS
   - Should we adopt this?
   - For which patients?
   - Implementation barriers?

8. DISCUSSION QUESTIONS (for teaching)
   - Knowledge-level questions
   - Comprehension questions
   - Application questions
   - Analysis questions
   - Synthesis questions
   - Evaluation questions
```
